About Me

Analytics creates real value when it is strategy- and product-driven. I like to work at the intersection of these, where data meets decisions.

I shine most when solving ambiguous business problems with data and first-principles thinking. Over time, I’ve realized that the hardest and most important step isn’t always building the right model, but asking the right questions. That’s where clarity, structure, and data-driven framing matter most—and where I thrive.

Professional Journey

I began my career at Delhivery with the Analytics & BI team, moved to Air India in the Operations Tech team, and now work in the Drug Development team at Bristol Myers Squibb, contributing to projects that improve patients’ lives.

Beyond Work

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy strength training, brewing coffee, and occasional blogging where I share both technical insights and personal reflections.
